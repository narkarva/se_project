Here are some of our policies, which we would appreciate if you abide by.

Rules for purchasing books

  • Once a book purchased, it cannot be returned.
  • While selecting number of copies, be careful, once payment done, cannot be refunded.
  • Do check the quality of book available before purchasing, no further changes will be entered.

Rules for renting books

  • A rented book, if not recieved back in proper condition will lead to fine.
  • Rent duration for rent amount paid will be explicitly mentioned in book information.
  • You can rent only one book at a time.
  • Home delivery is expected to reach within 2 working days. Rent period starts after you recieve delivery.
  • Keeping book for more days than rent duration will lead to fine.
